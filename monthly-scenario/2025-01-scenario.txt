### Dialogue 1: At the Office

1. Japanese (original script):

   A: "私と一緒に働いてくれてありがとう。"
   
   B: "いえいえ、こちらこそ。"
   
   A: "何時まで働いていますか？"
   
   B: "７時頃までです。"

2. Romaji (phonetic transcription):

   A: "Watashi to issho ni hataraite kurete arigatou."
   
   B: "Ie ie, kochira koso."
   
   A: "Nanji made hataraite imasu ka?"
   
   B: "Shichi-ji goro made desu."

3. English (translation):

   A: "Thank you for working with me."
   
   B: "No, thank you."
   
   A: "Until what time do you work?"
   
   B: "Until around 7 o'clock."

### Dialogue 2: Inside a House

1. Japanese (original script):

   A: "これはきれいですか？"
   
   B: "そうですね、キッチンカウンターはすっかりきれいになりました。"

   A: "何時に帰らなければなりませんか？"
   
   B: "今夜はいつでも帰れますよ。遅くなる必要はありません。"

2. Romaji (phonetic transcription):
   
   A: "Kore wa kirei desu ka?"
   
   B: "Sou desu ne, kitchen kauntaa wa sukkari kirei ni narimashita."
   
   A: "Nanji ni kaeranakereba narimasen ka?"
   
   B: "Konya wa itsu demo kaeremasu yo. Osoku naru hitsuyou wa arimasen."

3. English (translation):

   A: "Is this clean?"
   
   B: "Well, the kitchen counter is completely clean now."
   
   A: "What time do I have to leave?"
   
   B: "You can leave anytime you want tonight. There's no need to stay late."
