1. 今日は大晦日です。天気は寒くて曇りました。
2. Kyou wa oomisoka desu. Tenki wa samukute kumorimashita.
3. 
   - 今日は (kyou wa) = "Today" (particle は is used to indicate the topic of the sentence)
   - 大晦日です (oomisoka desu) = "is New Year's Eve" (です is the copula used to indicate the state or quality of something)
   - 天気は (tenki wa) = "The weather" (particle は is used to indicate the topic of the sentence)
   - 寒くて (samukute) = "cold and" (て form of adjective "cold" 寒い)
   - 曇りました (kumorimashita) = "was cloudy" (past form of verb "to be cloudy" 曇る)
   
   The first sentence describes the current date as New Year's Eve and mentions that the weather was cold and cloudy.

4. 
   - 今日 (kyou) = today (noun)
   - 大晦日 (oomisoka) = New Year's Eve (noun)
   - 天気 (tenki) = weather (noun)
   - 寒い (samui) = cold (i-adjective)
   - 曇りました (kumorimashita) = was cloudy (verb)

5. Original English Text:
"Today, is New Year's Eve. The weather was cold and cloudy.\nTomorrow will be New Year’s Day 2025."
