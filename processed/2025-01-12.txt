1. **Translation**:
洗濯機を修理しなければなりません。
息子に見せなければなりません。
私の携帯電話はどこですか？
晩ご飯は何がいいですか？

2. **Romaji**:
Sentakuki o shuuri shinakereba narimasen.
Musuko ni misenakereba narimasen.
Watashi no keitai denwa wa doko desu ka?
Bangohan wa nani ga ii desu ka?

3. **Sentence Breakdown**:
   - 洗濯機を修理しなければなりません。(Sentakuki o shuuri shinakereba narimasen.)
     - 洗濯機 (sentakuki) - noun meaning "washing machine."
     - を (o) - indicates the object of the verb.
     - 修理する (shuuri suru) - verb meaning "to repair," in its "must do" form しなければなりません (shinakereba narimasen), which expresses necessity.
   - 息子に見せなければなりません。(Musuko ni misenakereba narimasen.)
     - 息子 (musuko) - noun meaning "son."
     - に (ni) - indicates the target of the action.
     - 見せる (miseru) - verb meaning "to show," in its "must do" form 見せなければなりません (misenakereba narimasen), which expresses necessity.
   - 私の携帯電話はどこですか？(Watashi no keitai denwa wa doko desu ka?)
     - 私の (watashi no) - possessive pronoun "my."
     - 携帯電話 (keitai denwa) - noun meaning "cell phone."
     - は (wa) - topic marking particle.
     - どこ (doko) - adverb meaning "where."
     - ですか (desu ka) - question ending particle.
   - 晩ご飯は何がいいですか？(Bangohan wa nani ga ii desu ka?)
     - 晩ご飯 (bangohan) - noun meaning "dinner."
     - は (wa) - topic marking particle.
     - 何 (nani) - interrogative pronoun "what."
     - が (ga) - subject marking particle.
     - いい (ii) - i-adjective meaning "good."
     - ですか (desu ka) - question ending particle.

4. **Vocabulary List**:
   - 洗濯機 (sentakuki) - washing machine (noun)
   - 修理する (shuuri suru) - to repair (verb)
   - 息子 (musuko) - son (noun)
   - 見せる (miseru) - to show (verb)
   - 携帯電話 (keitai denwa) - cell phone (noun)
   - どこ (doko) - where (adverb)
   - 晩ご飯 (bangohan) - dinner (noun)
   - 何 (nani) - what (interrogative pronoun)
   - いい (ii) - good (i-adjective)

5. **Original English Text**:
I have to repair the laundry machine. 
I have to show my son. 
Where is my cell phone?
What would you like for dinner?
