1. **Translation**:
キッチンをきれいに掃除してもらえますか？
散歩に行きたいですか？
もっと枕が必要です。

2. **Romaji**:
Kicchin o kirei ni souji shite moraemasu ka?
Sanpo ni ikitai desu ka?
Motto makura ga hitsuyou desu.

3. **Sentence Breakdown**:
   - キッチンをきれいに掃除してもらえますか？(Kicchin o kirei ni souji shite moraemasu ka?)
     - キッチン (kicchin) - noun: "kitchen"
     - を (o) - particle: indicates the object of the action
     - きれいに (kirei ni) - adverb: "cleanly"
     - 掃除して (souji shite) - verb: "clean" (stem form)
     - もらえますか (moraemasu ka) - verb: "to receive" (potential form), question marker
     - か (ka) - particle: question marker
     - Translation: "Can you clean the kitchen nicely for me?"

   - 散歩に行きたいですか？(Sanpo ni ikitai desu ka?)
     - 散歩 (sanpo) - noun: "walking"
     - に (ni) - particle: indicates the purpose or direction of an action
     - 行きたい (ikitai) - verb: "want to go" (potential form)
     - です (desu) - copula: to be
     - か (ka) - particle: question marker
     - Translation: "Do you want to go for a walk?"

   - もっと枕が必要です。(Motto makura ga hitsuyou desu.)
     - もっと (motto) - adverb: "more"
     - 枕 (makura) - noun: "pillow"
     - が (ga) - particle: indicates the subject
     - 必要です (hitsuyou desu) - adjective: "necessary," (desu: polite form)
     - Translation: "I need more pillows."

4. **Vocabulary List**:
   - キッチン (kicchin) - noun: kitchen
   - きれいに (kirei ni) - adverb: cleanly
   - 掃除する (souji suru) - verb: to clean
   - もらう (morau) - verb: to receive
   - 散歩 (sanpo) - noun: walking
   - 行きたい (ikitai) - verb: want to go
   - もっと (motto) - adverb: more
   - 枕 (makura) - noun: pillow
   - 必要 (hitsuyou) - adjective: necessary

5. **Original English Text**:
Can you clean the kitchen?
Want to go for a walk?
I need more pillows.
