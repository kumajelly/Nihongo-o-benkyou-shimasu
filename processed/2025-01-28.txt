1. **Translation**:
後で電話します。
髪を切りたいです。
何を食べたいですか？

2. **Romaji**:
Ato de denwa shimasu.
Kami wo kiritai desu.
Nani wo tabetai desu ka?

3. **Sentence Breakdown**:
   - 後で電話します。(Ato de denwa shimasu.)
     - 後で (ato de) = later
     - 電話します (denwa shimasu) = will call
     - "Later, I will call (you)."
   - 髪を切りたいです。(Kami wo kiritai desu.)
     - 髪 (kami) = hair
     - 切りたいです (kiritai desu) = want to cut
     - "I want to cut (my) hair."
   - 何を食べたいですか？(Nani wo tabetai desu ka?)
     - 何 (nani) = what
     - 食べたいです (tabetai desu) = want to eat
     - か (ka) = question marker
     - "What do you want to eat?"

4. **Vocabulary List**:
   - 後で (ato de)
     - Meaning: later
     - Part of speech: adverbial phrase
   - 電話します (denwa shimasu)
     - Meaning: will call
     - Part of speech: verb
   - 髪 (kami)
     - Meaning: hair
     - Part of speech: noun
   - 切りたいです (kiritai desu)
     - Meaning: want to cut
     - Part of speech: verb
   - 食べたいです (tabetai desu)
     - Meaning: want to eat
     - Part of speech: verb
   - 何 (nani)
     - Meaning: what
     - Part of speech: pronoun

5. **Original English Text**:
I’ll call you later. 
I need a haircut. 
What do you want to eat?
